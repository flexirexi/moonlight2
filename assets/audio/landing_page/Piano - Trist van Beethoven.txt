Piano - Trist van Beethoven
A short sequence using triplets in a minor key—clearly inspired by Beethoven. This passage demonstrates that even in digital production, 
we fully utilize dynamics (velocity) and legato. Please understand that we are not presenting fully composed sonatas here.
